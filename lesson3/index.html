Site Porpuse

The main objective of the LasCondes.com website will be to help all SMEs to have a solid foundation of information they need to know in order to better develop the business they are building. This objective will be accomplished through the courses that have been developed by experts, which will be taught every week by one of these professionals. The program will provide information related to: Management, Transparency and Shareholder Relations, Control Systems, Personnel Training, Communication System, Compliance with Tax Obligations, Updated Financial Statements, and many more.

As a second purpose is to provide relevant information in terms of government support, this will depend on what the company's focus is, how many years it has been in operation and how many people it employs.
